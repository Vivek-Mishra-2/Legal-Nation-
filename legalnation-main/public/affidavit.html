Affidavit for Gap in education

I, Tushar Gupta s/o Hariom Gupta aged about 20 years r/o house no. 23 Barra, Kanpur, do hereby solemnly affirms and declares as under :-

1- That, I am resident of above said address.

2- That, I had passed 12th class in 2019 from XYZ School, Kanpur at age of 18 years.

3- That, I have not joined any college or university because I am preparing for JEE exam.

4- That, during this period I was not involved in any offence or in an illegal activities and that no criminal case is pending against me in any court of law.

5- That, I command a good reputation and respect in general public.

6- That, I have not availed any scholarship for the same programme name- B.A.LL.B(H) from any other institution or University.

Deponent

Verification

That the deponent verify that paragraph no. 1 to 6 are true and correct to my best knowledge. Nothing has been concealed.

Place:

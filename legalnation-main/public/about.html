About Us

We became fascinated by the idea of bringing the legal world to the light in form of case studies, i.e. providing the facility to the students to experience the practical and situational questions related to various legal facts that they face in the textual form. Besides, practicing in the courts, there is a better way to understand the world of Law & Order through real life examples, so that the students learn to implement what they have understood theoretically. This will help them in being vigilant practically and theoretically about the situational outlook of the legal scenarios and gives a mental exposure about the legal acts and orders.

Aim and Objective

  • By this initiative we are providing the facility to students to experience the practical and situational questions related to various legal Acts which they are studying in their text books.

  • By solving and studying these practical problems they will improve their logical and reasoning mind power which will help them to work more efficiently in future.

  • Our main aim is to make the students aware about the situational aspect and problems in legal field related to various act, and how to deal with the situational circumstances.

  • By this initiative we want that students should not only learn the theoretical approach of the law but also get aware about the practical application of the same.
